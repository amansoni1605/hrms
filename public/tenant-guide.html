User Roles & Access Levels

Super Admin

Full platform access. Manages all tenants, billing, DEK rotation, system health, and all HR functions. Typically the platform owner.

HR Admin

Full HR access within the tenant — employees, payroll, recruitment, onboarding, training, separation, analytics, and all settings.

HR Manager

Manages their team's attendance, leaves, expenses, and performance reviews. Cannot run payroll or access org-wide analytics.

Payroll Officer

Runs payroll, views all payslips and salary data, approves expenses. Cannot manage employees or HR configurations.

Finance Auditor

Read-only access to payroll, expenses, and salary data for auditing. Can approve expense claims at the finance stage.

Employee

Sees only their own data — profile, payslips, leaves, expenses, attendance, training, onboarding, and tax studio.

Recruitment

🎯

Job Openings

Create and manage open positions. Attach department, location, headcount, and salary band.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager
How to post a job
1
Go to Recruitment in the sidebar
2
Click New Opening → fill title, department, headcount
3
Set status to open to start receiving applicants
ℹ️Each opening tracks how many positions are filled vs. total headcount.
👥

Applicant Pipeline

Track candidates from shortlist → offer → hire. Kanban view per opening.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager
Hiring an applicant
1
Open the applicant's profile from the pipeline
2
Move them through stages: Shortlisted → Offer Extended → Offer Accepted
3
Click Hire — system creates employee record, login account, and onboarding checklist automatically
4
A temporary password (Welcome@XXXX) is shown — share it with the employee
✅Hiring is atomic — if anything fails, no partial records are created.

Onboarding

📋

HR View — Onboarding Tracker

Monitor all new hire checklists. Mark tasks complete on behalf of IT, manager, or HR.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager
Click any employee row to expand their checklist
Tick tasks to mark them complete — triggers status updates automatically
When onboarding is 100% complete, the employee is auto-enrolled in mandatory training
Training status (Enrolled / Completed) is visible in the expanded panel
ℹ️Default tasks cover: documentation, IT setup, orientation, compliance, and equipment.
✅

Employee View — My Onboarding

Employees see and complete their own assigned tasks with a live progress bar.

Who can access
Employee (own only)
Employee journey
1
Log in with the temp password provided by HR
2
Go to My Onboarding in the sidebar
3
Click each task assigned to you to mark it complete
4
When all tasks are done → Training programs unlock automatically
⚠️Tasks assigned to IT, HR, or Manager can only be completed by HR — employees see them as read-only.

Training & Development

📚

Training Programs

Create mandatory and optional programs. Track enrollment and completion per employee.

Who can access
HR Admin — full control Employee — My Training tab
First-time setup
1
Go to Training → All Programs
2
Click Seed Default Programs to add 12 standard programs (POSH, Code of Conduct, InfoSec, DEI, etc.)
3
Mandatory programs auto-enroll every new hire when their onboarding completes
Click View Enrollments on any card to see who enrolled and their completion status
Employees click Mark Complete on their My Training tab when they finish
When all mandatory programs are complete → employee is automatically marked Fully Ramped and status becomes Active
🗺️

Learning Paths

Group programs into ordered learning journeys by role or track.

Who can access
HR Admin
Go to Training → Learning Paths
Click New Path → name it and select programs in order
Assign a target role (e.g. "Engineer Onboarding Path")
ℹ️Learning paths help structure training for specific roles or departments but don't auto-enroll — enroll employees into the individual programs.

Payroll

💰

Payroll Runs

Monthly payroll calculation with statutory deductions, India tax regime support, and payslip generation.

Who can access
HR Admin Payroll Officer
Running payroll
1
Go to Payroll and click New Run
2
Select month/year — system pulls attendance, leaves, and deductions automatically
3
Review the run summary — check gross, deductions, and net totals
4
Click Approve & Run — payslips are generated for all active employees
5
Employees can view and download their payslip from My Payslips
⚠️Always run payroll after attendance is finalised for the month. Late clock-ins after payroll runs are not retroactively applied.
🧮

Tax Studio (Employee)

Employees choose between Old and New tax regime, enter 80C/80D investments, and simulate take-home pay.

Who can access
Every employee (own data)
Go to Tax Studio in the sidebar
Enter 80C investments (PPF, ELSS, LIC, etc.) and 80D premiums
System computes tax under both regimes and shows which saves more
§87A rebate and 4% cess applied automatically for AY 2026-27
✅Tax Studio is a simulation tool — declarations must be submitted separately to HR for Form 12BB processing.

Expense Claims

🧾

Expense Management

Employees submit expense claims; managers and finance approve in a two-step process.

Who can access
Employee — submit own HR Manager — stage 1 approve Finance Auditor — stage 2 approve
Approval flow
1
Employee submits from My Expenses with receipts and amounts
2
Manager approves (Manager Approve) — claim moves to finance queue
3
Finance Auditor does final approval and sets sanctioned amount
4
Finance marks as Paid after bank transfer
ℹ️Either stage can reject the claim. The employee is notified by in-app notification at every status change.

Performance Reviews

Who can access

1

Compensation Approvals

Two-step increment / promotion approval with skip-level and Segregation of Duties enforcement.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager (initiates)
Go to Performance → Comp Approvals
Initiator submits the change; a different approver must sign off (SoD rule — same person cannot initiate and approve)
Approved increments are effective-dated — salary updates on the specified date
Full compensation history is logged and visible to HR on the employee profile

Who can access
Employee — own goals HR Manager — team goals

Separation & Offboarding

🔚

Separation Management

Manage resignation, termination, retirement, and contract-end. Calculates Full & Final automatically.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager
Offboarding process
1
Click Initiate Separation → search for the employee by name
2
Select type (Resignation / Termination / Retirement / Contract End)
3
Set notice date and last working day
4
An offboarding checklist is created — IT, HR, Finance, and Manager tasks
5
Full & Final (F&F) is calculated: pending salary + leave encashment + gratuity − deductions
ℹ️Gratuity is calculated at 15 days' salary per year of service (threshold: 5 years, 26 working days/month).

Analytics & Burnout AI

📊

Organisation Analytics

Headcount, payroll totals, leave trends, and department-wise risk heatmap.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager Payroll Officer Finance Auditor
Summary cards: total employees, active, on leave, pending leave requests, latest payroll total
Risk distribution donut: High / Medium / Low burnout risk across the org
Department scatter: headcount vs. average burnout risk (spot problem departments)
6-month leave trend: approved vs. rejected leave requests over time
⚠️Payroll total shows ₹0 until your first payroll run is completed. Run payroll to see accurate figures.
🔥

Burnout AI

ML-derived burnout and flight-risk scores per employee. Department heatmap and watchlist.

Who can access
HR Admin HR Manager
High risk ≥ 0.7 — urgent attention needed
Medium risk 0.4–0.7 — monitor closely
Low risk < 0.4 — healthy
Watchlist shows employees who have shown a recent increase in risk score
ℹ️Risk scores are computed from attendance patterns, overtime, leave frequency, and performance signals. Scores update nightly.

Employee — First Login Checklist

Step 1 — Login
→
Use the temp password Welcome@XXXX shared by HR
→
Go to Settings → Security and set a new password immediately
Step 2 — Profile
→
Go to My Profile and verify your details are correct
→
Update mobile number and emergency contact if needed
Step 3 — Onboarding
→
Go to My Onboarding in the sidebar
→
Complete all tasks assigned to you — others (IT, HR) will complete theirs separately

→
Once onboarding is 100% done, go to Training → My Training
→
